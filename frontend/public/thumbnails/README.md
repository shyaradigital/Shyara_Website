# Portfolio Thumbnails

This directory contains thumbnail images for portfolio service cards.

## Required Thumbnails:

1. **smm-thumbnail.jpg** - Social Media Management thumbnail
2. **app-dev-thumbnail.jpg** - App Development thumbnail  
3. **video-reels-thumbnail.jpg** - Video Editing & Reels thumbnail
4. **ads-thumbnail.jpg** - Ad Campaign Management thumbnail
5. **festive-thumbnail.jpg** - Festive Posts thumbnail

## Image Specifications:
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Recommended Size**: 800x600px or 16:9 aspect ratio
- **File Size**: Keep under 200KB for optimal loading
- **Content**: Should represent the service visually (e.g., sample work, icons, or branded imagery)

## Note:
Replace the placeholder files with actual thumbnail images. The thumbnails will be displayed on the portfolio service cards for services that use Google Drive for their full portfolio.
